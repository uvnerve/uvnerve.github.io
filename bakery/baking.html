Cinnamon Swirls

Ingredients

For the Swirls
  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Demerara sugar
  • 1 tbsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 pack Ready to Roll Puff Pastry
  • 1 egg
  • Sugar for sprinkling (Optional)
For the Drizzle
  • 2 tbsp Icing Sugar
  • A few drops warm water (or lemon juice)
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 180° and line baking tray with baking paper.
  2. In a bowl mix the butter, sugar and cinnamon until well combined.
  3. Unroll puff pastry on its paper
  4. With a spoon spread cinnamon filling over pastery sheet leeaving a 1cm gap on a long edge
  5. Reroll pastry on its paper leaving the 1cm edge to seal swirls with.
  6. Using a sharp knfe, cut pastery roll into slices approx 2cm thick. Trimm ragged edges to give neat appearence and seal with strip left. Place on baking tray with a little space between each.
  7. Beat the egg and use lightly brush each swirl. If using, sprinkle with additional sugar and leave to rest from around 30 mins.
  8. Transfer to oven and bake for around 20 mins or until golden brown.
  9. Meantime, in small bowl, add icnig sugar with small quanity of warm water (or lemon juice if using), to make the drizzle.
  10. When ready remove swirls form oven. While still warm (not hot!) generously add drizzle to tops of the swirls.
  11. Transfere to wire rack to cool completly, before enjoying with your favourite beverage!

Bananna Loaf

Ingredients
  • 5oz Self Rasing Flour
  • 1/4tsp Bicarbonate of Soda
  • 1 Ripe Bananna
  • 1 Egg
  • 2oz Soft Butter
  • 5oz Sugar
  • 1/2tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4tsp Salt
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 150° and line small loaf with baking paper (or loaf case).
  2. Place egg, baannana, sugar, butter, vanilla extract and salt in liquidiser and blend on maximum to a smooth consistency.
  3. Sift flour and Bicarbonate of soda into mixing bowl and add the liquidised mixture.
  4. With electric mixter, whisk at low speed just long enough to thouroughly combine and carefullt spoon into loaf tin.
  5. Bake on middle shelf of oven for apporximatly 30 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven and allow to cool for several minutes before turning out on to a wire rack to cool completly.
  7. Enjoying with your favourite beverage!

Chocolate Biscuits

Ingredients
  • 225g (8oz) Plain Flour
  • 50g (2oz) Cocoa
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
  • 1 tbsp Instant Coffee
  • 175g (6oz) Soft Butter
  • 200g (7oz) Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Course Sugar (to coat)
Method
  1. Sift flour and cocoa into a bowl, add salt, ginger and coffee. Mix well and set aside.
  2. In second bowl, beat butter untill creamy, add brown sugar and beat more until pale and creamy.
  3. Add egg and vanilla extract then mix until just combimed. Place mixture on shert of baking paper, gather together and roll in the paper to make a 5cm diaminter sauasage.
  4. Roll the sauasage in cling film and chill for at least an hour. Preheat the oven to 200°. Unwrap dough, place on chopping board and leave for 5 mins. Roll in course sugar, pressing gently until coated.
  5. Using sharp knife, cut sauasage into slices around 5mm thick. Place well spaced on baking paper lied tray. Bake for 10 mins. Transfer to wire rack to cool.
  6. Enjoying with your favourite beverage!
