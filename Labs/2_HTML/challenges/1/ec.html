Edinburgh College

The Computer Networking HND I studied at Edinburgh College's Milton Road Campus; was full time and focused on vital areas of networking, security, troubleshooting, and administration. It also incororated both Microsoft Windows clinet/server systems and Linux Operating systems. Further studies included project management, forensics, Cisco routing, switching and wan teschnologies. The modules that comprised my award include:

First Year

  • Software Development (in C++)
  • Professionalism and Ethics in Computing
  • Building Local Area Networks
  • Project Management for IT
  • Troubleshooting Computing Problems
  • Computer Systems Fundamentals
  • Client Operating Systems
  • Team Working in Computing
  • PC Hardware ans Operating Systems
  • Networking Technology
  • Security Concepts
  • Graded Unit

Second Year

  • Routing Technology
  • Switching Technology
  • Managing a Webserver
  • Multi User Operating Systems
  • Computer Forensics Fundamentals
  • Internetworking Technology
  • Server Administration
  • Graded Unit Project

The current presentation of the course has been updated inline with evolving industry trends to include such topics as private cloud virtualisation and ethical hacking.
